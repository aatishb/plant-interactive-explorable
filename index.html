How do they find a ladder to the sky?

Well, they don’t have eyes… but being plants, they can tell light from dark, and somehow – we don’t know exactly how – they’re able to “see” – if that’s the right word – patches of light or dark around them. So this little vine senses a patch of darkness in any direction.

AHA! A shadow!

A shadow means something is blocking the sun. The bigger the shadow, the bigger that something is.
